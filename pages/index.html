OSSUI Home Page


Why do I need it? In today's increasingly interconnected world, cyber-attacks present a real threat to businesses of any size. Cyber attacks can cost businesses thousands for dollars and damages and many companies are not equipped to defend against them.

Who is it for? OSSUI is intended for small-businesses who may not have dedicated IT professionals. Therefore, it has been designed with ease-of-use in mind.

How can I get it? OSSUI can be downloaded directly from this site on the Download page. The OSSEC HIDS can be downloaded from OSSEC's site here. Instructions for installation are included with each download.
